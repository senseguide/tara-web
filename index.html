This isn't the horoscope you know.

Jyotish is Vedic astrology, thousands of years old, a science of light and time developed by Indian sages who observed the same stars you see tonight. It isn't a generic daily horoscope divided into twelve types. It is a precise system, built over centuries and practiced around the world.

How is it different from the astrology you grew up with?

Western astrology tracks the seasons, anchoring your sign to the calendar. Jyotish tracks the actual stars: the sky's real astronomical positions at the exact moment you were born. And where Western astrology centers on the Sun, Jyotish grounds itself in the Moon and your Janma Nakshatra, the precise imprint carried from your very first breath.

Unique to Jyotish are the Dashas: time cycles that divide your life into distinct chapters, each ruled by a different planet. A precise mathematical framework, refined over thousands of years.
